Tomato pasta

Ingredients - for 2 servings


  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 400g can cherry or chopped tomatoes
  • pinch sugar
  • 100g rigatoni or other tube-shaped pasta
  • 50g cheddar, grated

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a medium pan and fry the onion for 5 mins until softened and lightly golden.
  2. Stir in the garlic and cook for a further min. Mix in the tomatoes and sugar, bring to the boil and simmer for 20 mins.
  3. Meanwhile cook the pasta according to pack instruction.
  4. Drain the pasta and stir into the sauce.
  5. Scatter over the cheesy crumbs and grill for 3-5 mins or until bubbling hot and golden.
  6. Enjoy.
